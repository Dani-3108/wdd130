History

Founded in 2024 by the Osorio Family. This family has loved doing extreme activites since forever. But most of all, always loved to do extreme activites like razers on the beach, zip lining, parachute, bungie and a bunch of amazing things. While they were on their honey moon they tried for the first time the spinner rafting, it was a whole new experience for them and they loved it.

This event was the inspiration to fund their own company of extreme activites, specializing in Water Rafting. It took a lot of time looking for the right kind of rafting boat. They chosed the inflatable raft, they considered this kind of raft would be perfect for families, friends, couples or groups that would like to experience Water Rafting. And now these decisions has take them to have one of the most amazing Water Rafting companies in Alberta!
